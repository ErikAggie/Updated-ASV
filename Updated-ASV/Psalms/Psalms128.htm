A Song of Ascents.
1 Blessed is every one that feareth Jehovah,
That walketh in his ways.
2 For you shall eat the labor of your hands:
Happy shall you be, and it shall be well with you.
3 Your wife shall be as a fruitful vine,
In the innermost parts of your house;
Your children like olive plants,
Round about your table.
4 Behold, thus shall the man be blessed
That feareth Jehovah.
5 Jehovah bless you out of Zion:
And see you the good of Jerusalem all the days of your life.
