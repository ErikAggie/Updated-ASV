For the Chief Musician, set to Shoshanim Eduth. A Psalm of Asaph.
1 Give ear, O Shepherd of Israel,
You that leadest Joseph like a flock;
You that sittest above the cherubim, shine forth.
2 Before Ephraim and Benjamin and Manasseh, stir up your might,
And come to save us.
3 Turn us again, O God;
And cause your face to shine, and we will be saved.
 
4 O Jehovah God of hosts,
How long wilt you be angry against the prayer of your people?
5 You have fed them with the bread of tears,
And given them tears to drink in large measure.
6 You makest us a strife to our neighbors;
And our enemies laugh among themselves.
7 Turn us again, O God of hosts;
And cause your face to shine, and we will be saved.
 
8 You broughtest a vine out of Egypt:
You didst drive out the nations, and plantedst it.
9 You preparedst room before it,
And it took deep root, and filled the land.
10 The mountains were covered with the shadow of it,
And the boughs thereof were like cedars of God.
11 It sent out its branches to the sea,
And its shoots to the River.
12 Why have you broken down its walls,
So that all they that pass by the way do pluck it?
13 The boar out of the wood doth ravage it,
And the wild beasts of the field feed on it.
14 Turn again, we beseech you, O God of hosts:
Look down from heaven, and behold, and visit this vine,
15 And the stock which your right hand planted,
And the branch that you madest strong for yourself.
16 It is burned with fire, it is cut down:
They perish at the rebuke of your countenance.
17 Let your hand be upon the man of your right hand,
Upon the son of man whom you madest strong for yourself.
18 So will we not go back from you:
Quicken you us, and we will call upon your name.
19 Turn us again, O Jehovah God of hosts;
Cause your face to shine, and we will be saved.
