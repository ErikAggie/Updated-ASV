1 Jehovah reigneth; let the peoples tremble:
He sitteth above the cherubim; let the earth be moved.
2 Jehovah is great in Zion;
And he is high above all the peoples.
3 Let them praise your great and terrible name:
Holy is he.
 
4 The kingâ€™s strength also loveth justice;
You dost establish equity;
You executest justice and righteousness in Jacob.
5 Exalt you Jehovah our God,
And worship at his footstool:
Holy is he.
 
6 Moses and Aaron among his priests,
And Samuel among them that call upon his name;
They called upon Jehovah, and he answered them.
7 He spake to them in the pillar of cloud:
They kept his testimonies,
And the statute that he gave them.
8 You answeredst them, O Jehovah our God:
You wast a God that forgavest them,
Though you tookest vengeance of their doings.
9 Exalt you Jehovah our God,
And worship at his holy hill;
For Jehovah our God is holy.
