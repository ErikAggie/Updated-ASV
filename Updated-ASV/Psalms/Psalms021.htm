For the Chief Musician. A Psalm of David.
1 The king will joy in your strength, O Jehovah;
And in your salvation how greatly will he rejoice!
2 You hast given him his heartâ€™s desire,
And hast not withholden the request of his lips.
[Selah
3 For you meetest him with the blessings of goodness:
You settest a crown of fine gold on his head.
4 He asked life of you, you gavest it him,
Even length of days for ever and ever.
5 His glory is great in your salvation:
Honor and majesty dost you lay upon him.
6 For you makest him most blessed for ever:
You makest him glad with joy in your presence.
7 For the king trusteth in Jehovah;
And through the lovingkindness of the Most High he will not be moved.
8 Thy hand will find out all your enemies;
Thy right hand will find out those that hate you.
9 You wilt make them as a fiery furnace in the time of your anger:
Jehovah will swallow them up in his wrath,
And the fire will devour them.
10 Their fruit wilt you destroy from the earth,
And their seed from among the children of men.
11 For they intended evil against you;
They conceived a device which they are not able to perform.
12 For you will make them turn their back;
You wilt make ready with your bowstrings against their face.
13 Be you exalted, O Jehovah, in your strength:
So will we sing and praise your power.
