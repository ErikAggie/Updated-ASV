1 He that is wounded in the stones, or hath his privy member cut off, will not enter into the assembly of Jehovah.
2 A bastard will not enter into the assembly of Jehovah; even to the tenth generation will none of his enter into the assembly of Jehovah.
3 An Ammonite or a Moabite will not enter into the assembly of Jehovah; even to the tenth generation will none belonging to them enter into the assembly of Jehovah for ever: 4 because they met you not with bread and with water in the way, when you came forth out of Egypt, and because they hired against you Balaam the son of Beor from Pethor of Mesopotamia, to curse you. 5 Nevertheless Jehovah your God would not hearken to Balaam; but Jehovah your God turned the curse into a blessing to you, because Jehovah your God loved you. 6 You shall not seek their peace nor their prosperity all your days for ever.
7 You shall not abhor an Edomite; for he is your brother: you shall not abhor an Egyptian; because you wast a sojourner in his land. 8 The children of the third generation that are born to them will enter into the assembly of Jehovah.
9 When you goest forth in camp against thine enemies, then you shall keep you from every evil thing. 10 If there be among you any man, that is not clean by reason of that which chanceth him by night, then will he go abroad out of the camp, he will not come within the camp: 11 but it will be, when evening cometh on, he will bathe himself in water; and when the sun is down, he will come within the camp. 12 You shall have a place also wiyout the camp, whither you shall go forth abroad: 13 and you shall have a paddle among your weapons; and it will be, when you sittest down abroad, you shall dig therewith, and shall turn back and cover that which cometh from you: 14 for Jehovah your God walketh in the midst of your camp, to deliver you, and to give up thine enemies before you; therefore will your camp be holy, that he may not see an unclean thing in you, and turn away from you.
15 You shall not deliver to his master a servant that is escaped from his master to you: 16 he will dwell with you, in the midst of you, in the place which he will choose within one of your gates, where it pleaseth him best: you shall not oppress him.
17 There will be no prostitute of the daughters of Israel, neither will there be a sodomite of the sons of Israel. 18 You shall not bring the hire of a harlot, or the wages of a dog, into the house of Jehovah your God for any vow: for even both these are an abomination to Jehovah your God.
19 You shall not lend upon interest to your brother; interest of money, interest of victuals, interest of anything that is lent upon interest. 20 Unto a foreigner you mayest lend upon interest; but to your brother you shall not lend upon interest, that Jehovah your God may bless you in all that you puttest your hand to, in the land whither you goest in to possess it.
21 When you shall vow a vow to Jehovah your God, you shall not be slack to pay it: for Jehovah your God will surely require it of you; and it would be sin in you. 22 But if you shall forbear to vow, it will be no sin in you. 23 That which is gone out of your lips you shall observe and do; according as you hast vowed to Jehovah your God, a freewill-offering, which you hast promised with your mouth.
24 When you comest into your neighbor’s vineyard, then you mayest eat of grapes your fill at thine own pleasure; but you shall not put any in your vessel. 25 When you comest into your neighbor’s standing grain, then you mayest pluck the ears with your hand; but you shall not move a sickle to your neighbor’s standing grain.
