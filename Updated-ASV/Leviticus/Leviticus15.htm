1 And Jehovah spake to Moses and to Aaron, saying, 2 Speak to the children of Israel, and say to them, When any man hath an issue out of his flesh, because of his issue he is unclean. 3 And this will be his uncleanness in his issue: whether his flesh run with his issue, or his flesh be stopped from his issue, it is his uncleanness. 4 Every bed whereon he that hath the issue lieth will be unclean; and everything whereon he sitteth will be unclean. 5 And whosoever toucheth his bed will wash his clothes, and bathe himself in water, and be unclean until the even. 6 And he that sitteth on anything whereon he that hath the issue sat will wash his clothes, and bathe himself in water, and be unclean until the even. 7 And he that toucheth the flesh of him that hath the issue will wash his clothes, and bathe himself in water, and be unclean until the even. 8 And if he that hath the issue spit upon him that is clean, then he will wash his clothes, and bathe himself in water, and be unclean until the even. 9 And what saddle soever he that hath the issue rideth upon will be unclean. 10 And whosoever toucheth anything that was under him will be unclean until the even: and he that beareth those things will wash his clothes, and bathe himself in water, and be unclean until the even. 11 And whomsoever he that hath the issue toucheth, without having rinsed his hands in water, he will wash his clothes, and bathe himself in water, and be unclean until the even. 12 And the earthen vessel, which he that hath the issue toucheth, will be broken; and every vessel of wood will be rinsed in water.
13 And when he that hath an issue is cleansed of his issue, then he will number to himself seven days for his cleansing, and wash his clothes; and he will bathe his flesh in running water, and will be clean. 14 And on the eighth day he will take to him two turtle-doves, or two young pigeons, and come before Jehovah to the door of the tent of meeting, and give them to the priest: 15 and the priest will offer them, the one for a sin-offering, and the other for a burnt-offering; and the priest will make atonement for him before Jehovah for his issue.
16 And if any manâ€™s seed of copulation go out from him, then he will bathe all his flesh in water, and be unclean until the even. 17 And every garment, and every skin, whereon is the seed of copulation, will be washed with water, and be unclean until the even. 18 The woman also with whom a man will lie with seed of copulation, they will both bathe themselves in water, and be unclean until the even.
19 And if a woman have an issue, and her issue in her flesh be blood, she will be in her impurity seven days: and whosoever toucheth her will be unclean until the even. 20 And everything that she lieth upon in her impurity will be unclean: everything also that she sitteth upon will be unclean. 21 And whosoever toucheth her bed will wash his clothes, and bathe himself in water, and be unclean until the even. 22 And whosoever toucheth anything that she sitteth upon will wash his clothes, and bathe himself in water, and be unclean until the even. 23 And if it be on the bed, or on anything whereon she sitteth, when he toucheth it, he will be unclean until the even. 24 And if any man lie with her, and her impurity be upon him, he will be unclean seven days; and every bed whereon he lieth will be unclean.
25 And if a woman have an issue of her blood many days not in the time of her impurity, or if she have an issue beyond the time of her impurity; all the days of the issue of her uncleanness she will be as in the days of her impurity: she is unclean. 26 Every bed whereon she lieth all the days of her issue will be to her as the bed of her impurity: and everything whereon she sitteth will be unclean, as the uncleanness of her impurity. 27 And whosoever toucheth those things will be unclean, and will wash his clothes, and bathe himself in water, and be unclean until the even. 28 But if she be cleansed of her issue, then she will number to herself seven days, and after that she will be clean. 29 And on the eighth day she will take to her two turtle-doves, or two young pigeons, and bring them to the priest, to the door of the tent of meeting. 30 And the priest will offer the one for a sin-offering, and the other for a burnt-offering; and the priest will make atonement for her before Jehovah for the issue of her uncleanness.
31 Thus will you separate the children of Israel from their uncleanness, that they die not in their uncleanness, when they defile my tabernacle that is in the midst of them.
32 This is the law of him that hath an issue, and of him whose seed of copulation goeth from him, so that he is unclean thereby; 33 and of her that is sick with her impurity, and of him that hath an issue, of the man, and of the woman, and of him that lieth with her that is unclean.
